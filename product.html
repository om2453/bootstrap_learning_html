M8

M-series M8

The BMW M8 is a high-performance luxury sports car powered by a 625 hp twin-turbo V8, combining extreme performance with advanced M technologies, now discontinued as of 2026.

The BMW M8 is the high-performance version of the 8 Series, introduced in June 2019 under the BMW M sub-brand. Initially available as a 2-door coupe (F92) and convertible (F91), a 4-door Gran Coupe (F93) was added later in 2019 . The M8 was offered in standard and Competition variants, with the Competition model featuring increased engine power, a modified induction system, and more rigid engine mounts
